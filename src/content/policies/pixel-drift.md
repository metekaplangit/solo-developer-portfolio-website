Pixel Drift keeps your high scores on your own device. There are no accounts and
no online leaderboards during the beta, so the developer does not receive any of
your data.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking is used. If the game later adds online
features, this policy will be updated before those features ship, and the change
will be reviewed. Questions: **support@metkapstudio.com**. This policy is written in
plain language and is not legal advice.
