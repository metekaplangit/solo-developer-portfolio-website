Aurora Notes stores your notes on your device. It has no account system and the
developer never receives your note contents.

If you turn on sync, your notes are synchronized through **your own iCloud
account** using Apple's services; the developer has no access to that data and
does not operate any server that stores your notes.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking is used. Questions:
**support@example.com**. This policy is written in plain language and is not
legal advice.
