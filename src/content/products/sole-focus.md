Sole Focus is a quiet place to get things done. It pairs a classic Pomodoro
timer with a plain stopwatch, remembers the presets you reach for, and shows you
your focus adding up over time — without ever asking for an account or sending a
single byte off your Mac.

When you need to disappear into the work, **Zen mode** shrinks it to a small
always-on-top window, and **Study mode** leaves just a thin strip with one focus
quote. It's built local-first, so it's fast, works entirely offline, and keeps
your data yours.

_Currently in development — coming soon to the Mac App Store._
